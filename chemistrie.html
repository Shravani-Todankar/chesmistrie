— The Brand Story —

Five quiet chapters.
One bench.

Chemistrie was not founded in a boardroom. It was founded one milligram at a time, over a decade, on a single compounding bench in Houston.

  1. 2014 — I —

    The bench, on Heights Boulevard.

    Dr. A and Dr. M meet across the same compounding bench in Houston. A decade of weighing actives by the milligram begins — for patients, for partners, for the women they go home to.

  2. 2018 — II —

    The first wife. The first formula.

    Dr. M's wife asks for a retinol that does not burn. He compounds 0.05% retinaldehyde with niacinamide in a backroom mortar. Within four weeks, six other patients are asking for the same bottle.

  3. 2022 — III —

    The patient list becomes a waiting list.

    Friends of friends. A dermatology nurse's daughter. A schoolteacher in Austin. The bench cannot pour fast enough. The pharmacists begin labelling batches by hand, numbering each bottle as it leaves.

  4. 2025 — IV —

    The maison takes a name.

    A glassblower three blocks away. A letterpress in the same city. A photographer who shoots only in west afternoon light. The house is named on a Tuesday — Chemistrie, for the chemistry of patience, hands, and a woman's nightly ritual.

  5. 2026 — V —

    Today. And the women, still.

    Twelve formulas. Two hundred bottles a batch. A pharmacist on the phone, always. Chemistrie is the same bench it always was — only now, with a door open to you.
